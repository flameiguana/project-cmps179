Condition Matters

Price



In this project, we tried to visualize the relationship between product price and condition among some select categories of items on eBay. Our data visualization device of choice was to use a box-plot, since it is capable of representing products at different price ranges in a way that is easy to compare. To get more out of the typical box-plot, we also drew a line that visually shows difference in price. The various percentage markers can be clicked in order to visit the link of the specific product at that index.

We also plotted bid count per condition, and saw that for some categories, used items have more active bidding.

Usage:

  • For those who are unfamiliar with box plots, a diagram of the boxes has been provided.
  • Hover to the left or right edges of your browser to pop up a menu with the available categories.
  • Click on the bubbles in the box plot to go to the product page for the item at that percentile.
  • Hovering over the blue diagonal bars will tell you the percent difference between two prices or bid counts.
  • Click on "Prices" or "Bids" below the charts to change between these two data sets.
  • Hover over a box to set the values that are marked on the vertical axis.
